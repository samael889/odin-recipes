Easy Lasagna

This is an easy and quick recipe that will be sure to get some smiles (and seconds) around the dinner table.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound lea ground beef
  • 1 (32oz) jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces of cottage cheese
  • 3 Cups shredded mozzarella
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp Dried Parsley
  • Salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to Taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water
