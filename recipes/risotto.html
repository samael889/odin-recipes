Easy Risotto

An easy and delicious recipe for risotto.

This is and easy recipe that you and your family will love.

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons herbs de Provence
  • 2 1/4 cups vegetable broth, or as needed, divided
  • 2 cups white muchrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped leek
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 cup soy milk
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook rice and garlic in medium nonstick saucepan 2 minutes, stirring. Add 1/2 cup broth and cook until broth is absorbed.
  2. Add remaining broth, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until broth is absorbed before adding more broth. Cook until rice is done and creamy. Add cheese and pepper. Serve immediately.
